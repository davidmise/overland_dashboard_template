Logo Usage

Use the correct logo variant for each background. Never apply effects, change proportions, or place on non-approved backgrounds. Minimum height is 40px.

Logo-01 — Light / White background
Logo-02 — Dark / Black background
Logo-03 — Icon mark (collapsed sidebar)
Logo-04 — Black wordmark variant
Logo Do's & Don'ts: Never rotate, skew, recolour, add drop shadows, stretch, or place the logo on a busy background. Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the circular mark on all four sides. Minimum rendered height is 40px.
